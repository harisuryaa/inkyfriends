Privacy Policy for InkyFriends Mobile App

At InkyFriends, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application, InkyFriends.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when you create an account, such as your name and email address. We also collect message data that you submit through the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to provide you with access to our mobile application and its features, including connecting with your peers, staying updated about the latest events and news, and raising questions to verified people of the campus. We may also use your information to improve our mobile application and to communicate with you about updates and new features.

In addition, we use your information to provide you with personalized advertisements and to analyze user behavior and preferences in order to optimize our services.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our mobile application and its features, or as required by law. Clubs may generate content and moderate the app, but they will not have access to your personal information.

Security of Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect the security of your personal information, including using encryption and other industry-standard security measures. However, no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

In-App Chat Translator

Our in-app chat translator is a feature that allows users to translate messages in real-time. The feature works by sending the text of your message to a third-party translation service, which returns the translated text to us. We do not store the text of your messages or the translated text on our servers.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you by email or through our mobile application.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hari@inkyfriends.com.
